01 — About

I'm Aditya Raj — most people call me Radhe. I'm an AI engineer at IIT Patna, building RAG systems, autonomous agents, and LLM-powered products that solve real problems for real people.

Everything on this site was built from a tablet. No funding, no team, no fancy hardware. Just a stubborn belief that a single engineer with the right tools and the right questions can ship things that matter. The constraints became the edge — every architecture decision was forced to be lean, fast, and honest.

"The best way to predict the future is to build it — and the best way to build it is to stay curious about what came before."

I'm obsessed with the intersection of generative AI and Indian knowledge systems. I read the Bhagavad Gita in the morning and arXiv in the afternoon. I think the next decade of AI will be shaped by people who can hold both — and I'm trying to be one of them.

Off the clock, I trade Indian equities (poorly), study संस्कृतम् (earnestly), and argue about geopolitics with anyone who'll listen. I think intelligence, like attention, is something you choose to spend — not something you have.
